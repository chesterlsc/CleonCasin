# Prototype Instructions

Run the local server yourself and open the preview in the browser available to this environment. Do not give the user server-start instructions when you can run it.

Before making substantial visual changes, use the Product Design plugin's `get-context` skill when the visual source is unclear or no longer matches the current goal. When the user gives durable prototype-specific design feedback, preferences, or decisions, record them in `AGENTS.md`.

When implementing from a selected generated mock, treat that image as the source of truth for layout, component anatomy, density, spacing, color, typography, visible content, and hierarchy.

## Approved Blackjack Direction

- Use the selected Carbon Club Arena direction: charcoal and midnight navy, graphite felt, matte black rail, crisp white cards, coral primary action, mint secondary actions, and blue for the active human seat.
- Show the bankroll as a Philippine-peso balance. Do not use the phrase "practice credits."

- Casino chips must be highly visible, denomination-specific rendered assets with a clear add, undo, and clear flow; placed table wagers must use large physical chip stacks on desktop and mobile rather than tiny markers.
- Never expose the dealer's full hand total while the hole card is face-down. Show a neutral hidden state until the reveal sequence begins.
- Card dealing should feel theatrical and dimensional: stage cards from the shoe to each seat, stagger arrivals, flip the hole card, and animate dealer hits while respecting reduced-motion preferences.
- Brand the product and felt as `CLEOPATRA CASINO BLACKJACK` using the approved gold, platinum, and graphite C-and-crown emblem while preserving the graphite Carbon Club visual system. On mobile, use the approved full-width centered capsule: large emblem on the left, complete `CLEOPATRA CASINO` wordmark, `BLACKJACK` beneath it, and the three centered table-rule lines immediately below. Never reduce mobile felt branding to a small side emblem or compact corner lockup, and keep the complete composition clear of hands, wager circles, and controls. The long wordmark must scale without wrapping or crowding the capsule edge and retain generous right-side breathing room at every breakpoint.
- On mobile, give the felt the clear majority of the viewport. Keep the chip/deal dock short, place live Hit, Stand, Double, and Split decisions inside the lower table surface, and center the full CLEOPATRA capsule within the table with `BLACKJACK PAYS 3 TO 2` immediately below it.
- Support four functional Evolution-style side bets. 21+3 pays 5:1, 10:1, 30:1, 40:1, or 100:1; Perfect Pairs pays 6:1, 12:1, or 25:1; Hot 3 pays 1:1 for 19, 2:1 for 20, 4:1 for 21, 20:1 for suited 21, and 100:1 for 7-7-7; Bust It pays 1:1, 2:1, 9:1, 50:1, 100:1, or 250:1 for dealer busts using 3, 4, 5, 6, 7, or 8+ cards, and pushes when the player has Blackjack.

Mobile dragging must lift a visible chip under the finger and highlight valid drop targets. A side bet requires a main wager.
- Publish each player's running total as the opening cards physically arrive: show the first-card value after card one lands, then the full hand total after card two lands. Keep the dealer total hidden while the hole card is face-down.
- Settlement uses a compact, non-overlapping Evolution-style result banner only for positive results or Blackjack. It must not cover the player hand, wager rail, or controls; pushes and losses stay in the table status/toast layer without an interruptive popup. The banner shows the result title and total returned amount (original funded wager plus profit), never the PHP balance and never profit alone.
- A dealer win must remain in the table status layer only and must never create a floating toast over the deal/action controls.
- Mobile is a focused dealer-versus-player experience: show only the human seat and dealer and keep the primary controls in one viewport. Human-participating rounds do not spend deal-animation time on hidden AI seats, and no-wager solo autoplay uses that same single centered player seat.
- Deal pacing must be player-controlled with slow, normal, and turbo modes, and card arrivals should use a short physical swish-and-slap sound rather than generic electronic beeps.
- Keep side-bet circles grouped around the central main-bet circle, expose a persistent PHP wager ledger with total wins and net revenue, and provide both the six-seat 10-second arena and focused 12-second solo lobby.
- Match the approved live-table placement reference: the human hand sits directly above a horizontal Hot 3 / 21+3 / main-bet / Perfect Pairs / Bust It row, with the hand total in a light pill to the left of the cards and no duplicate player wager stack obscuring the row.

- In CLEOPATRA ONE, restart the full 12-second timer after every unresolved Hit or Split decision. In CLEOPATRA ROYALE, AI seats act one decision at a time with visible Hit/Stand feedback and a fresh 10-second window per decision.

## Approved Speed Baccarat Direction

- Treat `CLEOPATRA SPEED` as a first-class game beside CLEOPATRA ONE and CLEOPATRA ROYALE. The main entry view is the live-casino lobby so every game remains immediately discoverable.
- Preserve the Carbon Club graphite system and full CLEOPATRA branding. Use blue for Player, coral red for Banker, and warm gold for Tie, with the complete centered `CLEOPATRA CASINO · SPEED BACCARAT` felt capsule on desktop and mobile.
- Use an eight-deck shoe, a 12-second automatic betting window, and the standard Baccarat third-card tableau. Cards remain face up in normal play and use branded concealed backs when optional peel mode is enabled. The table must keep running spectator rounds when no wager is placed.
- Player pays 1:1, Banker pays 0.95:1 after commission, Tie pays 8:1 and pushes funded Player or Banker wagers, and Player Pair or Banker Pair pays 11:1.
- Support Player, Banker, Tie, Player Pair, Panda 8, Heavenly 9, Tiger 6, Tiger 7, and Banker Pair wagers with the shared visible chip denominations, tap placement, desktop drag-and-drop, per-spot clearing, undo, clear all, repeat, and Deal Now.

- On mobile, keep both hands, all nine wager spots, bead road, and compact chip/deal dock inside one viewport with no horizontal overflow. Result banners belong in the upper status zone and must never overlap cards, the center brand, wagering spots, or controls.
- Card and result audio must honor the shared sound toggle, and all deal/result motion must respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
- Baccarat should feel like a premium dimensional live table: a deep graphite felt bowl, layered matte rail, warm-metal edge highlights, real card depth, branded card backs, and crisp physical chip stacks. Preserve CLEOPATRA's identity while matching the visual polish and dramatic pacing expected from a top-tier live casino table.
